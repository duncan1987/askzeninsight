## 🎬 Launch Video Requirements

### Video Specifications

**Format & Hosting:**
- ✅ Must be hosted on **YouTube** (full URL, no shortened links)
- ✅ Video must be **public** (not private)
- ✅ Upload to YouTube **before** launch day
- ✅ Test that video is accessible

**Length:**
- **Recommended:** 30 seconds - 2 minutes
- **Sweet spot:** 45-60 seconds
- **Maximum:** 10 minutes (not recommended)

**Quality:**
- **Minimum resolution:** 1080p
- **Audio:** Clear, high-quality
- **Content:** Professional, clean footage

### Video Content Structure

**Script Template (60 seconds):**

```
[0:00-0:05] Intro: "Hi, I'm [your name], founder of Ask Zen Insight"
[0:05-0:15] Problem: "Many people struggle with anxiety, stress, and finding inner peace"
[0:15-0:30] Solution: "That's why I built Ask Zen Insight - an AI meditation teacher"
[0:30-0:45] Demo: "Show real conversation with koji, the AI teacher"
[0:45-0:55] Features: "Real-time streaming, fair usage, crisis detection"
[0:55-1:00] CTA: "Try it free at ask.zeninsight.xyz"
```

**Key Elements to Include:**
- ✅ Founder face/personality
- ✅ Real product demo
- ✅ Major differentiators
- ✅ Clear problem/solution
- ✅ Call-to-action

## 💬 Launch Day Engagement Strategy

### Responding to Comments

**For Positive Feedback:**
```
Thank you so much for the kind words! 🙏
Ask Zen Insight was built to make spiritual guidance accessible.
What features would you like to see next?
```

**For Questions:**
```
Great question! Yes, kami is trained in Zen philosophy and provides guidance using metaphors like water, clouds, and mirrors.
Try asking: "How do I deal with anxiety?"
```

**For Constructive Feedback:**
```
Thanks for the thoughtful feedback! 🤔
We're actually working on that feature for v2.0.
Want to be a beta tester when it's ready?
```

### Building Community

**Call to Actions:**
- "What's your favorite meditation practice?"
- "Have you tried AI for mental wellness before?"
- "What aspect of Zen interests you most?"
- "Share your experience with kami in the comments!"

**Engagement Tips:**
- Respond to every comment within 1 hour
- Ask follow-up questions to keep conversation going
- Share interesting user stories (with permission)
- Be authentic and humble

## 💡 Pro Tips from Successful Launches

1. **Launch on Tuesday-Thursday:** Product Hunt community is most active mid-week
2. **Time zones matter:** Launch at 12:01 AM PST (catch both US and Asia)
3. **Engagement trumps features:** Being responsive and authentic matters more than having every feature
4. **Video is critical:** Products with videos get 2.7x more upvotes
5. **Build pre-launch hype:** Share your product on social media before launch day
6. **Follow up:** Launch day is day 1, week 1 is where relationships are built
